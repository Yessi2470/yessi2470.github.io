Turismocity Redesign

Travel Platform Website Redesign
Travel Platform UX Research Website Redesign

Project Overview

Turismocity is a comprehensive travel booking platform that needed a complete user experience overhaul to improve conversion rates and user satisfaction. The existing platform suffered from navigation issues, unclear information architecture, and a booking flow that caused high abandonment rates.

This redesign project involved extensive UX research, competitive analysis, user journey mapping, and the creation of a more intuitive, conversion-focused travel booking experience.

Transforming Travel Planning

The redesign focused on simplifying the complex process of travel planning, making it easier for users to discover, compare, and book their ideal travel experiences.

Research & Problem Identification

Through user interviews, analytics analysis, and usability testing of the existing platform, I identified several critical pain points: confusing navigation structure, overwhelming search results, lack of clear pricing information, and a complicated booking process.

Users frequently abandoned their bookings due to uncertainty about pricing, difficulty comparing options, and a lack of trust in the booking process.

Before Redesign

  • • Confusing navigation
  • • Overwhelming search results
  • • Hidden pricing information
  • • Complex booking flow
  • • High abandonment rates

After Redesign

  • • Intuitive navigation system
  • • Clear, filterable results
  • • Transparent pricing display
  • • Streamlined booking process
  • • Improved conversion rates

Key Redesign Solutions

  • Simplified navigation with clear category organization
  • Enhanced search functionality with smart filters
  • Transparent pricing display with no hidden fees
  • Streamlined booking flow with progress indicators
  • Improved mobile responsiveness for on-the-go booking
  • Trust signals and security badges throughout the experience
  • Personalized recommendations based on user preferences
  • Clear cancellation and refund policy information

Design Process

The redesign process began with comprehensive user research, including surveys, interviews, and usability testing sessions. I created detailed user personas and journey maps to understand the complete travel booking experience from inspiration to post-trip follow-up.

The information architecture was completely restructured based on user mental models and task flows. Wireframes and prototypes were tested iteratively to ensure the new design addressed identified pain points.

User Experience Improvements

The new design prioritizes user confidence and clarity throughout the booking process. Key improvements include a simplified search interface, clear comparison tools, transparent pricing, and a step-by-step booking flow that reduces cognitive load.

Special attention was paid to mobile optimization, as research showed a significant portion of users were booking travel on mobile devices. The responsive design ensures a seamless experience across all device types.

Results & Impact

The Turismocity redesign successfully addressed the major usability issues identified in the research phase. The new design provides a clearer, more trustworthy booking experience that reduces user friction and supports business conversion goals.

This project demonstrates my ability to conduct comprehensive UX research, identify systemic usability issues, and design solutions that balance user needs with business objectives in the competitive travel industry.
