Turismocity Redesign

Travel Platform Website Redesign
Travel Platform UX Research Website Redesign

Project Overview

Turismocity is a travel booking website that needed a user experience overhaul. The original platform had usability issues such as unclear navigation, overwhelming search results, and a complicated booking process that led to high abandonment rates.

This redesign project focused on improving the desktop web experience, aiming for a cleaner and more trustworthy interface.

Research & Problem Identification

I conducted a UX audit that included usability testing, user feedback analysis, and a review of competitor platforms. This revealed several key issues:

  • ✓Confusing navigation structure
  • ✓Overwhelming and hard-to-filter search results
  • ✓Lack of transparent pricing
  • ✓Friction in the booking process

These usability gaps contributed to frustration and a high number of incomplete bookings, especially during checkout.

Before Redesign

  • Poor navigation structure
  • Cluttered search results
  • Hidden or inconsistent pricing
  • Complicated booking flow
  • Low trust and high drop-off rates

After Redesign

  • Clear and organized navigation
  • Search results with smart filters
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Streamlined booking journey
  • More focused and confident user experience

Key Redesign Solutions

  • Simplified navigation with well-defined categories
  • Enhanced search functionality with relevant filters
  • Clear pricing display throughout the flow
  • A linear, intuitive booking process
  • Visual trust signals like badges and reviews
  • Personalized suggestions based on user preferences
  • Clear policies for cancellation and refunds

Design Approach

I designed wireframes and high-fidelity mockups focused on improving usability and clarity. The information architecture was restructured to reflect user mental models, and design iterations were guided by usability insights.

Special attention was placed on building trust and reducing cognitive load during the booking process, helping users compare options and make decisions with ease.

Final Outcome

The redesigned desktop experience improves navigation, transparency, and booking efficiency. It offers users a smoother planning journey, from discovery to confirmation, aligned with modern UX best practices.
