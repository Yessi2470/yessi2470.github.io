QuickClean

Mobile Application Design
UX/UI Design User Research Mobile App

Project Overview

QuickClean is a mobile application designed to connect users with verified domestic cleaning professionals in Argentina. The app addresses the common challenge of finding reliable, trustworthy cleaning services while providing a seamless booking experience.

This project involved comprehensive user research, persona development, user journey mapping, and the creation of a complete mobile app interface from onboarding to service completion.

User Research & Problem Definition

Through user interviews and surveys, I identified key pain points in the current cleaning service market: lack of trust in service providers, difficulty in scheduling, unclear pricing, and limited communication channels between clients and cleaners.

The research revealed that users prioritized safety, reliability, and transparent pricing above all other factors when choosing cleaning services.

Key Features Designed

  • Comprehensive cleaner verification system with background checks
  • Real-time booking and scheduling interface
  • Transparent pricing calculator based on home size and services
  • In-app messaging system for client-cleaner communication
  • Rating and review system for quality assurance
  • Secure payment integration with multiple options
  • GPS tracking for cleaner arrival and service progress

Design Process

The design process began with extensive user research to understand the pain points and needs of our target audience. Through interviews and surveys, we identified key user needs and behaviors that would inform our design decisions.

Based on our research findings, we developed user personas and journey maps to guide the design process. This helped us create a user-centered interface that addresses real user needs and provides a seamless cleaning service booking experience.

Onboarding

Streamlined registration process with clear value proposition

Service Selection

Intuitive service customization with visual pricing feedback

Cleaner Profiles

Detailed profiles with ratings, reviews, and verification badges

Booking Flow

Simple scheduling with calendar integration and confirmation

Results & Impact

The QuickClean app design successfully addressed the identified user pain points through a clean, trustworthy interface that prioritizes safety and transparency. The design received positive feedback during user testing, with participants noting the app's professional appearance and ease of use.

The project demonstrated my ability to conduct thorough user research, translate insights into design solutions, and create a comprehensive mobile app experience that serves both service providers and consumers.
