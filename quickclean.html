QuickClean

Mobile Application Design
UX/UI Design User Research Mobile App

Project Summary

QuickClean is a personal UX/UI design project focused on solving the everyday challenge of finding trustworthy and professional domestic cleaners in Argentina. The concept was born from identifying a common need: simplifying and securing the hiring process through a user-friendly mobile application.

Problem & Approach

Many users struggle to find reliable cleaning services due to a lack of transparency, scheduling difficulties, and limited trust in unknown providers.
This project explored how design could reduce friction by improving trust, clarity, and convenience.

I defined the problem, objective, and solution early in the process, then conducted user research through interviews and surveys. The insights guided the creation of user personas, empathy maps, and user journeys.

Design Process

  • ✓Low- and high-fidelity wireframes
  • ✓Prototyping and user testing to refine the interface
  • ✓Information architecture focused on trust, simplicity, and control
  • ✓Designed the full user flow: onboarding → service selection → booking → reviews

Notable Features

  • ✓Cleaner profiles with verification indicators
  • ✓Transparent pricing calculator
  • ✓In-app scheduling and calendar integration
  • ✓Messaging system between users and professionals
  • ✓Secure, simple booking flow

Tools & Outcome

This project helped me grow as a UX/UI designer by applying a full design thinking process from scratch. Although it was not developed, the testing phase gave positive results and validated key design decisions.
