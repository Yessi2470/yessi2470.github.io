Beyond the Infinite

Company Landing Page
B2B Design Client Project Landing Page

Project Overview

Beyond the Infinite is a U.S.-based company that specializes in connecting exceptional Latin American talent with U.S. businesses. The challenge was to create a professional, trustworthy landing page that would effectively communicate their value proposition to potential clients and candidates.

As both a UX/UI Designer and Technical Recruiter at the company, I had unique insights into both the business needs and user pain points, which informed my design decisions throughout the project.

Design Challenge

The main challenge was to create a landing page that would appeal to two distinct user groups: U.S. businesses looking for talent and Latin American professionals seeking opportunities. The design needed to convey professionalism, trust, and the company's unique value proposition of bridging geographical and cultural gaps.

Key Design Solutions

  • Clean, professional layout that builds trust with corporate clients
  • Clear value proposition highlighting the benefits of Latin American talent
  • Dual-focused messaging for both businesses and candidates
  • Strategic use of testimonials and success stories
  • Mobile-responsive design for accessibility across devices
  • Clear call-to-action buttons for different user journeys

Design Process

Working closely with the business stakeholders, I conducted user research to understand the pain points of both target audiences. I created wireframes and prototypes, iterating based on feedback from both internal teams and potential users.

The final design successfully increased user engagement and conversion rates, helping the company establish a stronger online presence in the competitive talent acquisition market.

Results & Impact

The new landing page design contributed to improved user engagement metrics and helped establish Beyond the Infinite as a professional, trustworthy partner for businesses seeking Latin American talent. The clear, conversion-focused design supported the company's growth objectives during my tenure.
