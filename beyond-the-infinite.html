Beyond the Infinite

Company Landing Page
B2B Design Client Project Landing Page

Project Overview

Beyond the Infinite is a U.S.-based company that connects Latin American professionals with businesses in the U.S. The goal was to design a landing page that clearly communicated the company's purpose and built trust with both potential clients and candidates.

My Role

As the UX/UI Designer, I was responsible for the research, structure, and design of the website. My focus was on creating a clear, professional interface that supported the needs of two main user groups.

Design Challenge

The landing page needed to speak to two audiences at once:

  • ✓U.S. companies searching for talent
  • ✓Latin American professionals looking for job opportunities

The challenge was to make the message clear and relevant for both, while keeping the design clean and easy to navigate.

Key Design Decisions

  • Clean, trustworthy layout to support business credibility
  • Clear value proposition and dual messaging
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop
  • Strategic CTAs guiding both types of users
  • Visual hierarchy and testimonials to support the message

Design Process

I started by identifying the goals of the business and understanding both user profiles. I created wireframes and prototypes in Figma, iterated with feedback, and refined the layout to improve flow and clarity.
